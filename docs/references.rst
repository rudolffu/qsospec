References and Related Software
================================

The references below are grouped by the modelling components that appear in
``qsospec``: global continuum, Balmer continuum, Fe II pseudo-continuum,
broad-line profiles, line-complex recipes, and host-galaxy decomposition.

This list is not intended to be exhaustive. It highlights references that are
most relevant for the modelling choices implemented in ``qsospec``.

Method references
-----------------

General quasar spectra and spectral components
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

- `Francis et al. (1991), 1991ApJ...373..465F <https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/1991ApJ...373..465F/abstract>`__
  — early quasar composite spectrum and line measurements.

- `Boroson & Green (1992), 1992ApJS...80..109B <https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/1992ApJS...80..109B/abstract>`__
  — optical quasar spectral measurements, Eigenvector 1, and the classic
  I Zw 1-based optical Fe II template context.

- `Elvis et al. (1994), 1994ApJS...95....1E <https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/1994ApJS...95....1E/abstract>`__
  — multiwavelength quasar spectral energy distributions.

- `Vanden Berk et al. (2001), 2001AJ....122..549V <https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2001AJ....122..549V/abstract>`__
  — SDSS quasar composite spectrum.

- `Richards et al. (2006), 2006ApJS..166..470R <https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2006ApJS..166..470R/abstract>`__
  — quasar spectral energy distributions from optical to mid-infrared data.

- `Shang et al. (2011), 2011ApJS..196....2S <https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2011ApJS..196....2S/abstract>`__
  — quasar SED atlas and continuum diversity.

- `Krawczyk et al. (2013), 2013ApJS..206....4K <https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2013ApJS..206....4K/abstract>`__
  — large-sample quasar spectral energy distributions.

- `Euclid Collaboration: Lusso et al. (2024), 2024A&A...685A.108E <https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2024A%26A...685A.108E/abstract>`__
  — Euclid preparation study of active-galactic-nucleus spectroscopy with the
  NISP slitless spectroscopic mode.

- `Euclid Collaboration: Fu et al. (2025), 2025arXiv251208803E <https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2025arXiv251208803E/abstract>`__
  — Euclid quasar spectroscopy paper using early Euclid/NISP spectra, relevant

Balmer continuum and high-order Balmer lines
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

- `Grandi (1982), 1982ApJ...255...25G <https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/1982ApJ...255...25G/abstract>`__
  — classic AGN small-blue-bump / Balmer-continuum treatment.

- `Wills, Netzer & Wills (1985), 1985ApJ...288...94W <https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/1985ApJ...288...94W/abstract>`__
  — Fe II, Balmer continuum, and small-blue-bump modelling in quasars.

- `Storey & Hummer (1995), 1995MNRAS.272...41S <https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/1995MNRAS.272...41S/abstract>`__
  — hydrogen recombination line intensities, useful for high-order Balmer
  series templates.

- `Dietrich et al. (2002), 2002ApJ...581..912D <https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2002ApJ...581..912D/abstract>`__

- `Calderone et al. (2017), 2017MNRAS.472.4051C <https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2017MNRAS.472.4051C/abstract>`__
  — QSFit; includes automatic Balmer-continuum, iron, host, continuum, and
  emission-line fitting.

- `Vestergaard & Peterson (2006), 2006ApJ...641..689V <https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2006ApJ...641..689V/abstract>`__

Related software
----------------

Quasar/AGN spectral-fitting packages
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

- `PyQSOFit <https://github.com/legolason/PyQSOFit>`__
  — Python quasar spectral-fitting package derived from Yue Shen's IDL fitting
  code; includes continuum, Fe II templates, line-fitting parameter tables, host
  templates, and uncertainty estimation. See also
  `Guo et al. (2018) <https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2018ascl.soft09008G/abstract>`__
  and `Shen et al. (2019) <https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2019ApJS..241...34S/abstract>`__.

- `qsofitmore <https://github.com/rudolffu/qsofitmore>`__
  — extended PyQSOFit-family package for UV–optical quasar spectra, DESI/LAMOST
  use cases, Fe II/Balmer updates, and host-decomposition experiments; described in
  `Fu et al. (2022), 2022ApJS..261...32F <https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2022ApJS..261...32F/abstract>`__.

- `QSFit <https://github.com/gcalderone/qsfit>`__
  — IDL-based automatic optical AGN spectral-fitting code; simultaneously fits
  the global AGN continuum, Balmer continuum, Fe II complexes, host galaxy, and
  emission lines. See
  `Calderone et al. (2017) <https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2017MNRAS.472.4051C/abstract>`__.

- `QSFit.jl <https://github.com/gcalderone/QSFit.jl>`__
  — Julia reimplementation of QSFit with a native interactive GUI
  (`online demo <https://gcalderone.github.io/QSFit.jl/>`__).

- `BADASS3 <https://github.com/remingtonsexton/BADASS3>`__
  — Bayesian AGN Decomposition Analysis for SDSS Spectra; Python-based
  simultaneous AGN/host/line fitting with MCMC-oriented uncertainty estimation.
  See `Sexton et al. (2021) <https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2021MNRAS.500.2871S/abstract>`__.

- `FastSpecFit <https://github.com/desihub/fastspecfit>`__
  — DESI-oriented fast spectral synthesis and emission-line fitting. See
  `Moustakas et al. (2023) <https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2023ascl.soft08005M/abstract>`__.

- `SHEAP <https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.03934>`__
  — JAX/GPU-based AGN spectral-fitting framework described by Avila-Vera et al.
  (2026); continuum, host, Fe II, and multi-component emission-line fitting.
  A public repository link was not identified at the time this list was prepared;
  replace this link with the official repository if/when available.

Host-galaxy and stellar-continuum fitting
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

- `pPXF <https://github.com/micappe/ppxf>`__
  — penalized pixel-fitting code for stellar and gas kinematics, stellar
  populations, and full-spectrum fitting.

Template and line-list resources
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

- `Park optical Fe II template <https://github.com/DaeseongPark/Iron_Template>`__
  — optical Fe II template for the H beta region from Park et al. (2022).

General astronomy/spectrum infrastructure
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

- `Astropy <https://github.com/astropy/astropy>`__
  — core astronomy package for units, tables, FITS I/O, coordinates, and
  modelling utilities.

- `specutils <https://github.com/astropy/specutils>`__
  — Astropy-affiliated package for astronomical spectra.

- `speclite <https://github.com/desihub/speclite>`__
  — lightweight spectral utilities used in DESI-related workflows.

- `lmfit <https://github.com/lmfit/lmfit-py>`__
  — non-linear least-squares modelling interface built on SciPy optimizers.

- `emcee <https://github.com/dfm/emcee>`__
  — affine-invariant MCMC sampler often used for astronomical uncertainty
  estimation.
